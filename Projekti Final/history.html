Club History

125 years of excellence and tradition

1899

Foundation

FC Barcelona was founded by Joan Gamper and a group of Swiss, English, and Catalan footballers.

1957

Camp Nou Opens

The iconic Camp Nou stadium was inaugurated, becoming the largest stadium in Europe.

1992

First European Cup

Barcelona won their first European Cup at Wembley Stadium under Johan Cruyff.

2009

Historic Sextuple

Under Pep Guardiola, Barcelona achieved the unprecedented feat of winning six trophies in one year.

2015

Second Treble

Barcelona completed their second treble, winning La Liga, Copa del Rey, and Champions League.
