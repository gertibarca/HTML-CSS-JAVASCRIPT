The Eternal Rivalry

FC Barcelona vs Real Madrid - More than just a football match, it's a clash of cultures, identities, and football philosophies.

FC Barcelona

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is more than a club ("Més que un club") with a rich history of beautiful football and Catalan identity.

"We play football the way it should be played - with creativity, flair, and attacking intent."
- Johan Cruyff

Real Madrid CF

Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is synonymous with success, boasting numerous trophies and a global fanbase that echoes prestige and ambition.

"We are Real Madrid. Excellence, passion, and winning is our tradition."
- Alfredo Di Stéfano

History of the Rivalry

The El Clásico rivalry dates back to the early 20th century and symbolizes more than just football competition. It reflects political, cultural, and regional tensions between Catalonia and the Spanish capital. Matches are fiercely contested and watched by millions globally.
